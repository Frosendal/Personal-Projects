What is splitlist?

Splitlist is an App for your daily grocery-shopping. Create shoppinglists and easily share it with your friends and family from home! A friend or family member returning from work, school or hobby can shop for you on their way home without the cumbersome back and forth communication.

Not sure what's for dinner?

No worries! Splitlist got you covered with a whole forum of recipes from users around the world. Check out the forum right here.

Smarter than your average shoppinglist

The app does more than help you create and share shoppinglists, it even helps you keep track of what food you have at home. When you buy your groceries using Splitlist, the app updates your pantry automatically! You can also choose a delicious recipe from the forum, and the app will generate a shoppinglist based on your current pantry, saving you alot of unnecessary time cross checking kitchen drawers.

Minimizing your food waste

In 2020 more than 200,000,000 tons of food has been wasted worldwide. By generating shoppinglist based on what your pantry is missing to complete a recipe, Splitlist tries to empty your pantry and fight the global waste of food.
